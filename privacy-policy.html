Privacy Policy

Policy

Mukesh Hyundai has created this privacy policy to demonstrate its firm commitment to protecting your privacy. The following document describes our policies and practices regarding the collection, distribution and protection of information.



Compliance

Policy

By entering into a contract with Mukesh Hyundai, you agree to the terms of Mukesh Hyundai's Privacy Policy described below:

  • Collection of information: Mukesh Hyundai does not collect information about you or your organization other than what you have provided to us.
  • Use of information: Mukesh Hyundai collects this information to make better decisions for mutual benefit, to support the activities you choose, and to provide higher quality training and services. We do not sell or rent this information to any person or organization for commercial purposes.
  • Information security: Mukesh Hyundai classifies your personal information as confidential information. Confidential information is protected by storing it on secure servers that allow access only to Mukesh Hyundai staff and students. The data is encrypted to prevent unauthorized persons from accessing it.


Website Information Management Policy:

Policy

  • Our website may contain links to third party websites. Mukesh Hyundai does not control and is not responsible for the content, privacy practices or reliability of such websites.
